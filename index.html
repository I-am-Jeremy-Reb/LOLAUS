Spot Calculation — 80% Encircled Energy

\( E(r) = \displaystyle\int_0^r I(r')\, 2\pi r'\, dr' \quad\Rightarrow\quad r_{80} : E(r_{80}) = 0.8\, E_\text{total} \)

Upload a TIFF beam profile image. The algorithm computes the intensity centroid, then finds the radius containing 80% of the total encircled energy.

Gaussian Beam Propagation

\( w(z) = w_0\,\sqrt{1 + \!\left(\dfrac{z}{z_R}\right)^{\!2}}, \qquad z_R = \dfrac{\pi w_0^2}{\lambda} \)

Enter the beam waist w₀ and wavelength λ to compute the beam size w(z) at a distance z from the focus. The plot shows the full beam envelope.
